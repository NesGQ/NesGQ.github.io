## Volleyball

As most people in the world, I love football (a.k.a. soccer), but my love for volleyball is unparalleled. A beautiful sport I actively played as a teenager and later in life when I did my master's and PhD in the UK. Intensity, discipline and teamwork that I hope I can bring into research and teaching. A quick introduction and some of the best games I have watched so far:
